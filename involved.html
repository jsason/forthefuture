Conservation International

For the past 30 years, Conservation International has been focused on protecting nature and promoting sustainable economies. So far, they have helped protects more than 6 million square kilometres of land and sea across more than 70 countries.

Rainforest Alliance

From fighting deforestation and climate change to building economic opportunities and better working conditions for rural people, the Rainforest Alliance is working to solve urgent environmental and social challenges.

WildAid

WildAid works to reduce global consumption of wildlife products and to increase local support for conservation efforts. They also work with governments to protect marine reserves from illegal fishing and shark finning, to enhance public and political will for anti-poaching efforts, and to reduce climate change impacts.

Friends of the Earth

Friends of the Earth strive to make healthy food available to all, promote clean energy solutions, and empower people to hold organizations accountable for destroying rainforests. They push for reforms that are needed and prioritize a healthy and just world.

Simple ways to go green at home:

1. Switch the lightbulbs around your house to energy efficient types.

2. Replace old appliances to energy efficient models.

3. Start using a reusable water bottle and cloth bags.

4. Wash laundry in cold water instead of hot.

5. Take public transport more often, or combine errands so you won’t have to make multiple trips in a car.

6. Shop at local farmer’s markets.

7. Check Craigslist or Ebay before buying something new.

8. Collect rainwater to water houseplants.

9. Pay your bills online to reduce paper waste.

10. Plant a simple backyard garden. .
