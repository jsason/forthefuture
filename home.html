About the Archive

Posted by J Sason, January 8, 2021

    Welcome to for the future. This archive was created to serve as a beginners guide to the countless environmental concerns spoken about in the media. I wanted to make that information accessible to all ages by using simple, everyday language. Coming across posts by environmentalists can be overwhelming, especially when they use academic language and assume the reader has some background knowledge. I understand that not everyone is an expert on these issues and am here to provide the basics for you. Whether you’re in high school working on a project or an adult wanting to strengthen your understanding on a specific topic, I hope to provide the basics to you.

    If there is a topic which you would like covered, feel free to send me a message by clicking the mail icon below! I'll try to to update the archive as quick as I can.
